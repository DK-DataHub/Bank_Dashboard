# Banking Analytics Dashboard

This project showcases a comprehensive **Banking Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI** to analyze customer behavior, credit distribution, and retention trends. The dashboard integrates multiple datasets (Customers, Accounts, Loans, Repayments) and provides interactive insights for decision-making in the banking sector.

### Key Features
- **Customer Segmentation**: Active vs. inactive customers, retained vs. exited customers.
- **Credit Analysis**: Distribution by credit score categories (Excellent, Good, Fair, Poor).
- **Exit Trends**: Monthly exit patterns, gender-based exit analysis, and credit card impact.
- **Location Insights**: City-wise breakdown of customer activity (New York, Chicago, Dallas, Seattle, San Francisco).
- **Interactive Filters**: Gender, status, credit type, location, year, month, and exit/retained status.

### Tools & Skills Demonstrated
- **Power BI**: Data modeling, DAX measures, interactive dashboards.
- **Data Cleaning & Transformation**: Preparing customer and repayment datasets for analysis.
- **Visualization Techniques**: Line charts, bar charts, pie/donut charts, stacked visuals.
- **Business Insights**: Identifying retention challenges and credit risk patterns.

### Outcomes
- Built a recruiter-friendly, professional dashboard highlighting **customer retention challenges** and **credit risk distribution**.
- Demonstrated ability to translate raw banking data into **actionable insights** for business strategy.
